# Pulse Pro - Custom Workout Plans with AI

## Overview

Welcome to Pulse Pro! Pulse Pro is an innovative gym app that leverages the power of AI to create personalized workout plans tailored to your fitness goals, preferences, and progress. This README file provides an overview of the app, instructions for setup, usage, and other important information.

## Features

- **AI-Generated Workout Plans**: Personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and preferences.
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your progress with detailed statistics and analytics.
- **Exercise Library**: Access a comprehensive library of exercises with instructions and video demonstrations.
- **Adaptable Routines**: Workout plans adapt based on your progress and feedback.
- **Community Support**: Connect with other users, share experiences, and get motivated.
- **Integration with Wearable Devices**: Sync data from popular fitness trackers and smartwatches.

## Installation

### Prerequisites

- iOS 12.0 or later / Android 6.0 or later
- Stable internet connection

### Steps

1. **Download the App**: Get Pulse Pro from the [App Store](https://www.apple.com/app-store/) or [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store).
2. **Install**: Follow the on-screen instructions to install the app on your device.
3. **Sign Up**: Create an account using your email or social media accounts.

## Usage

### Getting Started

1. **Create Your Profile**: Enter your personal information, fitness goals, and preferences.
2. **AI Setup**: Allow the AI to analyze your data and generate a custom workout plan.
3. **Start Your Workout**: Follow the plan provided, watch exercise demonstrations, and log your activities.

### Key Features

- **Workout Dashboard**: View your daily workout plan, upcoming exercises, and progress.
- **Exercise Library**: Browse exercises by category, muscle group, or equipment.
- **Progress Tracking**: Check your performance metrics, such as weight lifted, distance run, and calories burned.
- **Feedback and Adjustment**: Provide feedback after each workout to help the AI adjust your future plans.

## Customization

- **Plan Adjustments**: Manually adjust your workout plan if needed.
- **Preferences**: Update your preferences for exercise types, workout duration, and intensity.
- **Goals**: Set new fitness goals and track your achievements.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

- **App Crashes**: Ensure you have the latest version of the app. Restart your device and try again.
- **Sync Problems**: Check your internet connection and make sure your wearable device is properly paired.
- **Login Issues**: Reset your password or contact support if you're unable to log in.
